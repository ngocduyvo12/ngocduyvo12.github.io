Ngoc Vo

About Me

My name is Ngoc Vo, I have a bachelor degree in Mechanical Engineering at University of Colorado Denver. I initially started out as a Bioengineering major, but as a new immigrant with limited understanding of English, it proved to be quite a challenge. Between that and knowing the fact that I enjoy solving analytical problems finally convinced me to change my major. And I have to say it is probably one of best decision I have made in my life. I really enjoyed learning about all aspect of mechanical engineering.

During my senior year, I was able to get an internship with AMP robotic as an intern engineer. After my graduation, they offered me a job right away as a robotic technician. I though it would be a great chance for me to get out on to the field and apply what I have learned in a real-world environment. Worked started out fine and I learned a lot of useful technique, and I get to travel a lot. But after a while, I wanted to spend more time with my family and my current position does not allow me to do so. Coding has always been a passion of mine, during my time as a student, I would often spend time teaching myself python for my senior design project. And I find myself liking coding more and more. So I started to get into coding and I was lucky to be able to get into University of Denver’s full-stack coding bootcamp.
